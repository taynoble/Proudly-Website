03 / Fundraising — The Everywhere Card

The card that gives back every time it's spent.

The Everywhere Card is a spend-capture fundraising model built for K-12 schools, educational organizations, and nonprofits. Every time a supporter uses their card, 3% flows right back to the cause.

Rebated back
3%
How it fundraises

One card. Every purchase. Ongoing support.

The Everywhere Card flips the traditional fundraiser on its head. Instead of selling wrapping paper or candy bars once a year, your community gets a digital Visa® or Mastercard® card branded to your organization — and 3% of every dollar spent on it is rebated back.

  • Spendable anywhere Visa® or Mastercard® are accepted.
  • 3% of every card load returns to your organization.
  • Digital-first — no physical cards to print, ship, or lose.
  • Branded to your school, team, or nonprofit.
Who it empowers

Built for the people who give their time.

Families, students, donors, board members, volunteers — the Everywhere Card is easy for everyone to use, and every use adds up.

K-12 schools

Fund the classroom, athletics, arts, and trips families care about.

Educational organizations

Alumni groups, booster clubs, PTAs — sustained, effortless giving.

Nonprofits & causes

A fresh channel that doesn't compete with your major-gift program.

The mechanism

Three steps. No selling. No friction.

Most traditional fundraisers ask volunteers to become salespeople. The Everywhere Card simply asks them to spend like they already do.

01

Load the card

Supporters purchase an Everywhere Card branded to your organization — any denomination from $10 to $1,000.

02

Spend anywhere

Use it for groceries, gas, travel, gifts — anywhere Visa® or Mastercard® are accepted. Digital, instant, safer than cash.

03

Rebate back

3% of the card value is automatically rebated back to your organization. No invoices, no chasing, no hassle.
