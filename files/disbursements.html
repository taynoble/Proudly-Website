The fastest way to pay. The smartest way to trace.

Issuing a check takes days. Wiring a payment takes fees and friction. Proudly delivers a branded digital card instantly — and unlike cash, every dollar is traceable from issue to redemption.

  • Instant issuance — funds in hand in seconds.
  • 100% spend transparency, card by card.
  • Category-level spend controls for mission-critical programs.

Need AID

Aid that arrives in seconds — and stays on mission.

Our Need AID program is built for the moments when every hour matters. Relief organizations, mutual aid networks, and social service teams use Proudly to put funds directly in the hands of people who need them — without the overhead of cash logistics or the risk of fraud.

  • Bulk issue thousands of cards from a single upload.
  • Restrict spend to relevant merchant categories (groceries, fuel, pharmacy).
  • Reclaim unused balances automatically.
  • Full audit trail for funders, boards, and regulators.
