About Proudly®

A payment platform with a pulse.

We build the infrastructure that lets organizations recognize people, grow brands, fuel causes, and disburse funds — all with the same care a handwritten thank-you would carry.

Why we exist

Money, sent well, becomes more than money.

It becomes recognition. Loyalty. Relief. Opportunity.

Proudly is how organizations move money the way they mean it.

What we believe

The principles under every card we issue.

01

Frictionless by default.

A great reward or a timely payment shouldn't require a user manual. Every interaction with Proudly — from issue to redemption — is designed to feel effortless for everyone involved.

02

Transparent by design.

You should see where every dollar goes. We built Proudly so finance teams, funders, and boards never have to wonder about the journey of a payment.

03

Built for social good.

The Proudly+ and Everywhere Card programs make generosity automatic. We don't treat impact as a marketing feature — we treat it as infrastructure.

04

No hidden fees.

Zero card issue fees. You pay only for the value loaded on each card. Denominations from $10 to $1,000. It's that simple, and we'd like to keep it that way.

05

Security first, always.

Branded, trackable, and recoverable — Proudly cards are engineered so you get speed and control, not speed at the expense of control.

06

Human support, real people.

Our team is fully staffed to answer questions from cardholders, admins, and partners. Email us anytime at info@proudlygive.com — a person reads it.

Meet the founders

Three decades of finance. One clear mission.

Proudly was founded by a father-and-son team with deep roots in payments, banking, and fintech — and a shared belief that moving money well should feel like a gift, not a burden.
